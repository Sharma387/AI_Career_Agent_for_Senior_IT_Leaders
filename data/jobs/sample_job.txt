====================
FinServe Global is a Series D fintech company ($2.8B valuation) transforming how institutional investors access and manage alternative assets. Our platform processes $40B in monthly transaction volume across 2,000+ institutional clients in 35 countries. Backed by Sequoia Capital, Andreessen Horowitz, and Tiger Global, we're building the next-generation infrastructure for global finance. Our engineering culture prioritizes reliability, security, and performance — because in our world, downtime isn't an inconvenience, it's a regulatory and financial risk.

ROLE OVERVIEW
=============

You'll lead a 65+ person engineering organization across Platform, SRE, Data Infrastructure, and Developer Experience teams. This is a high-impact leadership role reporting directly to the CTO with visibility to the board and executive leadership team. You'll be responsible for ensuring our platform meets the stringent reliability, security, and performance requirements of institutional financial services while enabling engineering velocity across 8 product squads.
